Cold Coffee

Description

There are so many ways to jazz up a simple glass of cold coffee. I am sharing the most basic version here.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder
  • Packet of full fat whole milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Heat up 1/4 cup milk in a pan. Add instant 2 tablespoons instant coffee.
  2. Add in 3 tablespoons sugar..
  3. Mix everything together using a whisk.
  4. Add to blender.
  5. Add 2 cups of chilled milk to blender. Add 3 ice cubes.
  6. Blend until mixed and frothy.
  7. Serve and garnish with chocolate syrup.
