Iced Tea

Description

Crisp and refreshing homemade iced tea is easy to make and easy to scale up for a crowd. This is always a hit at summer gatherings. Serve it with your favorite sweetener or enjoy it unsweetened.

Ingredients

  • Tea
  • Cold Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Brew the tea in a saucepan. Bring half the water to boil and remove it from heat.
  2. Add in the tea bags and allow them to steep for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove tea bags from the hot water.
  4. If you want sweetened tea, add the sugar and whisk until it is dissolved.
  5. Add the cold water.
  6. Serve.
